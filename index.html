Markets Served

Powering Critical
Industries Worldwide

Our mineral products serve as essential inputs across a broad spectrum of industrial applications.

🏗️
Construction & Cement

Silica and calcium carbonate as key functional additives in cement, concrete mixes, and construction materials for improved durability.

🩺
Pharma & Personal Care

Ultra-pure amorphous silica used as excipients, anti-caking agents, and functional fillers in pharmaceutical and cosmetic formulations.

🖨️
Paints, Coatings & Inks

High-purity silica and calcium carbonate as matting agents, extenders, and rheology modifiers in industrial and decorative coatings.

🔋
Rubber & Polymers

Precipitated silica as a reinforcing filler in green tire manufacturing, industrial rubber compounds, and polymer composites.

🌾
Agriculture

Biochar for soil amendment, enhanced water retention, carbon sequestration, and improved crop yields in sustainable farming.

💧
Water Treatment

Biochar and silica-based media for water filtration, contaminant adsorption, and industrial effluent treatment systems.

🧴
Food & Feed

Food-grade silica as an anti-caking agent in powdered foods, feed additives, and food processing applications.

⚡
Electronics & Energy

High-purity silica for advanced electronics manufacturing, solar panel encapsulants, and energy storage applications.
